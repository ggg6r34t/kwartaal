Motion like a well-made drawer

Physical, brief, and only where it carries meaning. Nothing loops, nothing bounces for joy, nothing moves while you read. The one indulgence: closing a quarter feels like a drawer settling shut.

1 · The drawer settles

drawerSettle · 420ms · standard · once

Fires exactly once, when "I filed it" + "I paid it" are both checked. Drops from −18px, overshoots +3px at 65%, rests. The 3px overshoot is the weight of the drawer — remove it and the moment dies.

stepCollapse · 240ms · standard

3 · Term drawer opens

termIn · 180ms · standard · exit 120ms fade

Tapping a dotted term raises the glossary card from the corner: 10px rise + scale 0.98→1. Quick enough to feel like a footnote, not a modal. Escape or × closes with a plain 120ms fade — exits never animate position.

4 · Set-aside fill & explain layer

fillGrow 420ms · explainIn 260ms

Bars fill once on first paint (never on re-render). Turning the learn layer on lifts each aside in with a 6px rise — turning it off is instant, no exit animation.
