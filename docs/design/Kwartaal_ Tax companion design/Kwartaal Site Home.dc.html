Vocabulary

Learn the Dutch words as you go

The official forms are in Dutch — so Kwartaal keeps the Dutch terms, explains each one the first time, and dots it ever after. By your second quarter, the form reads like yours.

The btw you already paid on business costs. You reclaim it — it reduces what you pass on to the Belastingdienst.
