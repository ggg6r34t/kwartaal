The decision
What you gain

Your 2026 income tax spread over monthly payments of about €1.280 — no single large bill months after the year ends.

What you lose

Flexibility. Overpay and the refund waits until after filing; underpay and a top-up still comes. You must update it if income shifts.

Our read  Usually good once income is steady — yours is. Your Taxes pot already covers the discipline; this just automates the paying.
